BREAKDOWN OF VIRAT KOHLI'S CENTURIES

Test Centuries


Virat Kohli has scored more than 27 centuries in Test cricket. His highest individual score in Test matches is 254* against South Africa in Pune in October 2019.

ODI Centuries


As of my last update, Kohli has scored over 70 centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs). His highest individual score in ODIs is 183 against Pakistan in an Asia Cup match played in Dhaka on March 18, 2012.
