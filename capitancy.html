VIRAT'S LEADERSHIP ROLE

Indian Test Team


Virat Kohli took over the captaincy of the Indian Test team from MS Dhoni in 2014. Under his captaincy, the Indian Test team achieved significant success, including notable series victories both at home and abroad.

Indian Limited-Overs Team


Kohli also led the Indian limited-overs teams, including One Day Internationals (ODIs) and Twenty20 Internationals (T20Is) for a considerable period. However, in September 2021, he announced that he would step down as the captain of the T20I team after the T20 World Cup 2021.

Royal Challengers Bangalore (IPL)


Virat Kohli has been associated with RCB since the inception of the IPL in 2008. He took over the captaincy of RCB and has been leading the team in the IPL. Under his captaincy, RCB has seen both highs and lows in different seasons.
